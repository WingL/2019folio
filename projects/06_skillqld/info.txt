About the project
Promoting a free Certificate III of Individual Support course. This course was promoted through the community by the following item:
Newspaper - With QR which direct to the course web page. Published in Northside Chronicle, Wednesday August 10, Page 12
Poster - Posted on wall in the centre and local shops
Web Banner - Published on NAC's homepage
Banner - Display in local library

About the course
The course was hosted by Nundah Activity Center with TAFE teaching and qualification. It was funded and supported by the Queensland Government under Skilling Queenslanders for Work program.
