About the project
Promoting a free Certificate III of IT course. This course was promoted through the community by the following item:
Newspaper - With QR which direct to the course web page. Published in Northside Chronicle, Wednesday 1 June, Page 35
Poster - Posted on wall in the centre and local shops
Brochure - With detail information of the course. Available in the centre
Web Banner - Published on NAC's homepage

About the course
The course was hosted by Nundah Activity Center. It was funded and supported by the Queensland Government under Skilling Queenslanders for Work program.
