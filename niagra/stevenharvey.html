Steven Harvey

1965 Born Sydney, Australia

Steven Harvey is a young artist to watch out for. Since his first exhibitionin 1994 Harvey abstract coastal landscapes have taken the art world by storm.

Harvey first solo exhibition in Melbourne opens at Niagara Galleries this Tuesday 29 May and it is a must see!

Harvey lives in Sydney, and spends a great deal of time walking along the dramatic coastal area of the Botany Bay National Park. This new series of paintings is inspired by walks along the high, rocky cliffs the Great Ocean Road in Victoria. Harvey paints the meeting of sea, sky and land with broad, expressive brushstrokes. Thickly applied paint, in a multitude of colour, oozes over the edge of every stretcher.

Steven Harvey is continuing a long tradition of Australian artists interested in the language of the landscape; he is extending that tradition by giving us a different way into and onto the landscape we all experience and inhabit.?(Peter Sharp, 2000)

Harvey has a Masters of Art (painting) and was an artist-in-residence at the Arthur Boyd Bundanon Trust in 1997
